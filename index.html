What Can Vibing Do? Vibing 能做什么？

Clean Speech
What you said
I think we should, um, move the meeting to, uh, Thursday because, you know, everyone is, like, like, busy on Wednesday.
What Vibing types
I think we should move the meeting to Thursday because everyone is busy on Wednesday.
你说的
我喜欢的食物有...呃...苹果、嗯...香蕉、还有那个...我想一下...对，还有梨
Vibing 输出

Vibing strips filler words, false starts, and verbal tics — you get clean text, not a transcript. Vibing 自动去除口语填充词和冗余表达，输出干净文字而非逐字转录。

Changed your mind mid-sentence? Vibing catches self-corrections and keeps only your final intent. 说到一半改了主意？Vibing 捕捉自我纠正，只保留最终意图。

Video Introduction 视频介绍

Privacy 隐私

Vibing sends audio and a screenshot of your active window to the cloud for processing. Both are deleted immediately after — nothing is stored, nothing is used for training. Vibing 将音频和当前窗口截图发送到云端处理。处理完成后立即删除——不存储，不用于训练。

Screenshots are used solely for context-aware formatting — helping Vibing understand whether you're in an email, a document, or a chat. 截图仅用于上下文感知格式化——帮助 Vibing 理解你是在邮件、文档还是聊天中。

Your Control您的控制权
You can disable screenshot capture in Settings. Without it, Vibing still works — just without context-aware formatting and language adaptation. 您可以在设置中关闭截图功能。关闭后 Vibing 仍然可用，只是没有上下文感知的格式调整。
