How Hotkeys Work

Three modes — Transcribe, Translate, and ESC to cancel anytime

Toggle Mode

Transcribe — Press to Start / Stop

Press Right ⌥ Option (Mac) or Ctrl + Win (Win) once to start recording. Press again to stop. Your speech is transcribed, polished, and auto-copied to clipboard.

Hold Mode

Transcribe — Hold to Record, Release to Transcribe

Long-press the hotkey and hold it down. Recording stays active while key is held. Release to stop and transcribe. Content is auto-copied to clipboard on completion.
